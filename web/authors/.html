Four Sentences that Lead to Wisdom

topics:

These come from author Louise Penny, and are passed on by her detective protagonist, Armand Gamache, in several of her books.

  1. I am sorry.
  2. I was wrong.
  3. I don’t know.
  4. I need help.

The Rotarian Four-Way Test

topics: ethics

The Four-Way Test is a nonpartisan and nonsectarian ethical guide for Rotarians to use for their personal and professional relationships.

The test is widely promoted as one of the guiding principles of Rotary International.

Of the things we think, say or do:

  1. Is it the truth?
  2. Is it fair to all concerned?
  3. Will it build goodwill and better friendships?
  4. Will it be beneficial to all concerned?
